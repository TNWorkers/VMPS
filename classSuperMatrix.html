auxiliary dimension of an Mpo / MpoQ / SuperMatrix:
- Daux=1: local operator
- Daux=2: sum of local operators
- Daux=3: Ising

- Daux=5: Heisenberg, XXZ
- Daux=6: Hubbard, Kondo
- Daux=7: extended Hubbard
