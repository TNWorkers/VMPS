Models » Kondo Models

Detailed Description

Kondo Model.

MPO representation of

where further parameters from Hubbard and Heisenberg are possible.

Parameters
D: where is the spin of the impurity.
Note
Take use of the U(1) particle conservation symmetry.
The U(1) symmetry is borken due to the field in x-direction.
The default variable settings can be seen in KondoU1::defaults.
is antiferromagnetic
If nnn-hopping is positive, the GS-energy is lowered.
The multi-impurity model can be received, by setting D=1 (S=0) for all sites without an impurity.
